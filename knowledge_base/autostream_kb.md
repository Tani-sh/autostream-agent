# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview

AutoStream is a SaaS platform providing automated video editing tools for content creators. It uses AI to automatically cut, trim, add captions, and optimize videos for social media platforms. AutoStream eliminates hours of manual editing so creators can focus on what matters — making great content.

## Getting Started

1. Sign up at autostream.io (no credit card required for trial).
2. Connect your YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or other social account.
3. Upload your raw footage (MP4, MOV, AVI supported).
4. AutoStream processes and edits your video automatically using AI.
5. Preview, tweak if needed, then download or publish directly from the dashboard.

## Use Cases

- **YouTube Creators**: Reduce editing time from hours to minutes for long-form content.
- **Instagram & TikTok**: Quickly generate Reels and short-form clips optimized for vertical formats.
- **Businesses**: Create regular marketing videos without needing a video editor.
- **Podcasters**: Convert audio recordings into engaging video content with captions.
- **Educators**: Produce course videos and tutorials with automated captions for accessibility.
